## 📌 Project Overview

This end-to-end **Business Intelligence Dashboard** was built using **Microsoft Power BI**, leveraging advanced **Data Analysis** and **Time Series Forecasting** techniques to generate actionable insights from Super Store sales data.

The dashboard empowers business stakeholders to:
- 📈 Monitor real-time KPIs across sales, profit, and orders
- 🌍 Identify regional and category-level performance patterns
- 🔮 Forecast next **15-day sales** with high accuracy
- 💡 Make data-driven decisions to optimize revenue and operations

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

### 💳 1. Payment Mode Analysis
| Mode | Share |
|------|-------|
| 💵 Cash on Delivery (COD) | 43% |
| 🌐 Online Payments | 35% |
| 💳 Cards | 22% |

> **Insight:** COD dominates — invest in digital payment incentives (discounts, cashbacks) to shift customers to online/card payments for better cash flow management.

---

### 🗺️ 2. Regional Sales Performance
| Region | Contribution |
|--------|-------------|
| 🏆 West | 33% |
| 📍 East | 29% |
| 📍 Central | 22% |
| 📍 South | 16% |

> **Insight:** West region leads — South has significant growth potential. Targeted marketing campaigns in South & Central can unlock untapped revenue.

---

### 🛍️ 3. Top Performing Categories
| Category | Revenue |
|----------|---------|
| 🥇 Office Supplies | $0.64M |
| 🖥️ Technology | $0.47M |
| 🪑 Furniture | $0.45M |

> **Insight:** Office Supplies dominates. Consider bundling with Technology products for cross-sell opportunities.

---

### 🚢 4. Shipping Preferences
| Ship Mode | Volume |
|-----------|--------|
| 📦 Standard Class | 0.33M |
| 📬 Second Class | 0.11M |
| ✈️ First Class | 0.08M |
| ⚡ Same Day | 0.03M |

> **Insight:** Standard Class is the go-to — optimize logistics partnerships for this tier. Premium shipping upsell for high-value orders.

---

### 📅 5. Monthly Sales & Profit Trends (2019 vs 2020)
- 📉 **Sales dip observed in April** — potential seasonal pattern requiring promotional intervention
- 📈 **Year-end surge (Oct–Dec)** — holiday season drives peak performance
- 🔄 **YoY growth confirmed** — 2020 outperformed 2019 across most months

---

### 🎯 6. Top Sub-Categories by Sales
| Sub-Category | Revenue |
|-------------|---------|
| 📱 Phones | $0.20M |
| 🪑 Chairs | $0.18M |
| 📁 Binders | $0.17M |

---

### 🔮 7. 15-Day Sales Forecast
> **Predicted Average Daily Sales: $5,304**

Built using **Power BI's built-in Time Series Forecasting**, the model analyzes historical trends to project the next 15 days, enabling proactive inventory management and staffing decisions.

## 📐 Dashboard Features

- ✅ **Dynamic KPI Cards** — Total Sales, Quantity, Profit, Avg Ship Days
- ✅ **Interactive Filters** — Region-wise drill-through (Central / East / South / West)
- ✅ **Donut Charts** — Payment Mode, Segment, and Region breakdowns
- ✅ **Bar Charts** — Ship Mode and Category comparisons
- ✅ **Line Charts** — Month-wise Sales & Profit trends (2019 vs 2020)
- ✅ **Map Visual** — Geographic Sales & Profit heatmap by State
- ✅ **Sub-Category Bar Chart** — Revenue by product sub-type
- ✅ **15-Day Forecast Visual** — Future sales prediction with confidence bands
